Message from the Missionaries

Hello again Kansas City 3rd Ward!

We hope you all had a wonderful Thanksgiving week and were able to enjoy the blessings that we all have received. We have reflected a lot on gratitude this last week and how much it can make our lives better. When we are grateful for what we have then it is easier to concentrate on the positives in life. Truly, the Lord blesses us every minute of every day with air to breathe, eyes to see, and much much more. When we look for the positives and the blessings in our lives we are able to show a spirit of optimism and know that the Lord will provide. We will also grow in our desire to be a blessing to others and uplift and encourage them.

In Mathew chapter 10, Christ commissioned the twelve Apostles to go and teach with the counsel, "...freely ye have received, freely give."

At the beginning of this Christmas season may we all remember that counsel and look for ways to share the blessings we have received.

Thank you again for all you do!
    Read Matthew 10
